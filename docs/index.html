About me

Hi! My name is Katie and I'm currently a senior at University of California, Berkeley. I'm currently pursuing a double major in Compuer Science, and Molecular and Cell Biology with emphasis on Biochemistry and Molecular Biology (Track 1). I often introduce myself as a Korean from Canada studying in the US, but with my graduation rolling around the corner in Spring 2021, I'm sad to say that I no longer will have that phrase to introduce myself.

In terms of my CS background, I really enjoy web development as my current and previous internships have been creating web apps from scratch for the companies I worked for. I've also had the opportunity to dabble a little bit in computer graphics through coursework: CS184. Combined, I've been able to make cool graphics projects that you can see in my Projects page as well as making this website alone was a fun side project I did during Fall 2020.

Additionally, I'm interested in projects that lie at the intersection of biology and technology, and I'm excited that I'm working as a software developer currently for a biotech startup at Berkeley SkyDeck that has cryopreservation technology for Fall 2020.

When I'm not working on projects for school, clubs, work, or personal fun, you can catch me outside playing golf, or watching YouTube videos on movie recaps. I also play the cello and love playing in ensembles—small and large!
