About me

Hi! My name is Katie and I'm currently a senior at University of California, Berkeley. I'm pursuing a double major in Compuer Science, and Molecular and Cell Biology with emphasis on Biochemistry and Molecular Biology (Track 1). I often introduce myself as a Korean from Canada studying in the US, but with my graduation rolling around the corner in Spring 2021, I'm sad to say that I no longer will have that phrase to say about myself.

I really enjoy (full stack) web development, as my current and previous internship projects were creating complete web applications from scratch for the companies I worked for. I've also had the opportunity to dabble a little bit in computer graphics through coursework in Summer 2020—CS184: Foundations of Computer Graphics. Combined, I've been able to make cool graphics projects that you can see in my Projects page, and making this website itself was a fun side project I did during Fall 2020.

Additionally, I'm interested in projects that lie at the intersection of biology and technology, and I'm excited that I'm working as a software developer currently for Fibulas, Inc., a biotech startup focussing on cryopreservation technology, at Berkeley SkyDeck.

When I'm not working on projects for school, clubs, work, or personal fun, you can catch me outside playing golf, or watching YouTube videos on movie recaps. I also play the cello and love playing in ensembles—small and large!
